Reveal: This is a simulated delivery.

In real scams, those "followers" are usually fake/bot accounts or accounts obtained through follow-exchange networks. Real Instagram engagement will not increase, and Instagram periodically purges fake accounts.

  • Red flags: guaranteed followers, instant delivery, cheap mass packages, no refunds or lots of fine-print.
  • Risks: chargebacks, account flags, wasted money, damaged credibility.

How scammers typically operate (explain in your video)

  1. Bots & fake accounts: Sellers control many disposable accounts that follow or like posts. These accounts don’t bring real engagement.
  2. Follow/unfollow networks: Real people or low-paid workers use follow-for-follow systems; they often unfollow shortly after.
  3. Resellers & recycled accounts: Accounts may be sold multiple times or rented — unstable and risky.
  4. Chargebacks & refunds: Buyers often dispute payments; sellers disappear or provide smoke-and-mirror excuses.
