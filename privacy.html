1) Who we are & scope

Controller: ScriptNova. We operate the Allstars mobile apps and website and any feature that links to this Policy.

  • Contact gomegaassist@gmail.com
  • Applies to visitors, registered users, creators, and others interacting with the Service.

2) Information we collect

Below are the main categories of data we process. Some items are optional or depend on how you use Allstars.

2.1 Account & profile

  • Identifiers: email, username/handle, display name, user ID, authentication tokens, verification status (e.g., blue check at ≥35k followers or discretionary verification).
  • Profile: avatar image/URL, bio, links, privacy setting (public/private), counts (followers, following, likes, views).
  • 3rd-party sign-in: if you choose Google (or similar), we receive identifiers and tokens necessary to authenticate you. We don’t receive your Google password.

2.2 User-generated content & social graph

  • Videos, images, captions, tags/hashtags, comments, likes, follows, blocks, reports, and moderation outcomes.
  • Content metadata: file type (e.g., .mp4/.mov), resolution, duration, upload time, thumbnails, view counts, and engagement statistics.

2.3 Device, app & network

  • Device data: model, OS/version, unique device identifiers (e.g., IDFV), language, time zone, app build version.
  • Network/diagnostics: IP address, approximate location inferred from IP, user agent, referrer, crash logs, performance metrics, and error traces.

2.4 Usage & interactions

  • Session activity: screens/pages viewed, taps/clicks, search terms, watch time, dwell time, skips, completion rate, replay rate, and other engagement signals used to operate recommendations and safety systems.
  • Settings: autoplay, mute, notification preferences, private account, blocked words, and similar.

2.5 Device permissions (if you enable them)

  • Camera/Photos/Microphone: to record or upload content and set avatars.
  • Notifications: for alerts (e.g., likes, follows, comments, policy updates). You can disable these in system settings.
  • Approximate location: derived from IP; we don’t require precise GPS.

2.6 Payments & purchases

Allstars presently does not process payment card data directly. If we introduce purchases or subscriptions, payments will be handled by platform providers (e.g., Apple/Google). We will update this Policy before launching those features.

2.7 Communications

  • Support requests, emails, bug reports, survey responses, and feedback, including metadata like timestamps and delivery/open rates for service emails.

2.8 Sensitive categories

We do not seek sensitive data (e.g., health, biometric, racial/ethnic origin) and ask that you do not include it in your content or profile. If sensitive data appears (e.g., in a video you upload), processing is incidental to providing the Service (hosting, display, moderation).

3) Where we get information

  • You (directly through the app/website).
  • Automated means (cookies/SDKs, logging, analytics).
  • Other users (e.g., reports, mentions, collaborations).
  • Third parties (sign-in providers; safety & anti-abuse tools; analytics providers).

4) How we use information

  • Operate the Service (create/manage accounts, host/stream content, remember preferences, maintain uptime and performance).
  • Personalize & recommend content (e.g., “For You” vs “Following”), rank feeds, suggest creators, optimize relevance and discovery.
  • Safety & integrity (detect spam, fraud, policy violations; enforce community rules; protect against harmful or illegal content).
  • Analytics & research (feature performance, engagement, A/B tests, understanding what improves user experience).
  • Communications (service announcements, policy updates, support replies; optional marketing with consent where required).
  • Legal & compliance (respond to lawful requests, resolve disputes, enforce our Terms, maintain records).

5) Automated decision-making & profiling

We use automated systems to rank/recommend content, detect spam/abuse, and prioritize moderation queues. These systems use signals such as follows, watch time, completion rate, repeats, skips, likes, hides/reports, and device/network heuristics. You can influence these outcomes by your actions (e.g., following creators, liking videos, using a private account). Where legally required, you may object to certain forms of profiling; see “Your rights”.

6) Legal bases (GDPR/UK GDPR)

  • Contract – to provide the Service you request (account, uploads, streaming, core features).
  • Legitimate interests – personalization, safety/anti-abuse, analytics, product improvement, and fraud prevention (balanced against your rights).
  • Consent – certain cookies/SDKs, marketing emails, optional features.
  • Legal obligations – record-keeping, law enforcement requests, and compliance with applicable laws.
  • Vital interests – rare cases to protect a person’s safety.

7) How we share information

  • Service providers (processors) – cloud hosting, storage/streaming, authentication, analytics, customer support, security & anti-abuse. They act under contract and may use data only as instructed.
  • Public/audience visibility – your profile (name/handle, avatar, bio, follower/following counts, verified badge), and content you post are visible according to your settings (public vs private).
  • Business transfers – in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, data may be transferred as permitted by law.
  • Legal – to comply with laws, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and the integrity of Allstars and our users.
  • With consent – we may share in other ways with your explicit consent.

We do not sell personal information for money. If we ever “share” data for cross-context behavioral advertising (CPRA), you will have the right to opt out; see U.S. addendum.

8) Personalization, analytics & advertising

  • Personalization – used to tailor feeds and creator suggestions. You can influence results by following/unfollowing and using private accounts.
  • Analytics – we use privacy-respecting analytics to understand usage and improve Allstars.
  • Advertising – if we introduce ads or measurement, we will update this Policy and provide required controls (opt-outs where applicable and Global Privacy Control/GPC signals where recognized).

10) Data retention & deletion

  • Account & profile – kept while your account is active. On deletion, we delete or de-identify within a reasonable time unless we must retain some data (e.g., legal compliance, fraud prevention, dispute resolution).
  • Content – kept while posted; removed when you delete it or delete your account, subject to caching/backups for a limited time.
  • Logs/analytics – kept for operational and security needs for a limited period (e.g., up to 12–24 months) and then deleted or aggregated.

You can request deletion or export of your data; see “Your rights & controls”.

11) Security

  • Transport encryption (HTTPS), access controls, least-privilege access, secret management, and routine patching.
  • Passwords are stored using industry-standard hashing methods provided by the sign-in provider (e.g., Firebase Auth).
  • No system is 100% secure. You are responsible for safeguarding your login credentials and reporting suspected compromise promptly.

12) International transfers

We may process data in countries other than yours (e.g., the U.S.). Where required, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses and transfer risk assessments).

13) Your rights & controls
